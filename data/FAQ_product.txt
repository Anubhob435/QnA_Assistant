Company FAQ - Product Specific (Insight Analyst) 
Insight Analyst - Product Frequently Asked Questions 
• Q1: What is Insight Analyst? Insight Analyst is a powerful data analytics platform 
designed to help businesses transform raw data into actionable insights. It offers features 
like data visualization, trend analysis, and predictive modeling. 
• Q2: What types of data sources can Insight Analyst connect to? Insight Analyst can 
connect to a wide variety of data sources, including SQL databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, 
MySQL), NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB), cloud storage services (e.g., AWS S3, 
Google Cloud Storage), and direct file uploads (CSV, Excel). 
• Q3: Is there a free trial available for Insight Analyst? Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial 
for Insight Analyst. You can sign up on our website to explore its features with your own 
data. 
• Q4: What are the system requirements for Insight Analyst? Insight Analyst is a web
based platform, so a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and a stable 
internet connection are required. For on-premise deployments, specific server 
requirements will be provided based on the expected data volume and user load. 
• Q5: How often is Insight Analyst updated? We release minor updates and bug fixes for 
Insight Analyst on a bi-weekly basis. Major feature releases typically occur quarterly.
